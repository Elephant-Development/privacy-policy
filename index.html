We value your privacy and are committed to safeguarding your information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal data when you interact with our website and services through Facebook and Google APIs.

1. Information We Collect

When you interact with our website or services through Facebook’s or Google’s API, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Facebook Profile Information: If you log in using Facebook or connect your account, we may collect basic profile details such as your name, email address, and profile picture.
  • Google Profile Information: If you log in using Google or connect your account, we may collect basic profile details such as your name, email address, and profile picture.
  • Group Post and Comment Data: When our service posts content or comments in Facebook groups or Google groups on your behalf, we may collect data about the posts, comments, and interactions.

We use the information collected via Facebook and Google APIs for the following purposes:

  • Group Posts: To make posts in Facebook and Google groups on your behalf, including content sharing and interactions.

  • Chat Interactions: To send or receive messages via Facebook’s and Google’s messaging system, including customer support, automated responses, or other group-related communication.
  • Personalization: To enhance your experience, providing tailored content and interactions through Facebook and Google.
  • Communication: We may use your contact details to send updates, promotional messages, or responses related to your group activities, posts, comments, or chats.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. However, your data may be shared under the following circumstances:
